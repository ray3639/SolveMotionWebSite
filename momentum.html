Momentum 5.0 — A quiet check-in for your day.

Momentum is not another loud fitness app. It is a calm place to notice how your body and brain feel over time so you can change things before they break.

What Momentum tracks

  • Daily energy and stress levels.
  • How much you sit, move, and recover.
  • Simple notes around sleep, work, and pain.

Why it matters

  • Small posture and movement changes add up.
  • Burn-out rarely happens overnight — patterns show early.
  • Seeing your week at a glance makes decisions easier.
