Pricing

Solve Motion's apps are meant to be simple to access. Final pricing may change, but the spirit is to keep things affordable for students and everyday users.

Momentum

  • One-time purchase or simple subscription.
  • No ads, no data selling.
  • Built for long-term use, not quick clicks.

Solvelt

  • Access to a growing library of cases.
  • Optional packs for specific topics (bias, evidence, interviews).
  • Designed so teachers can use it as a learning tool.
