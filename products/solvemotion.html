SolveMotion

Intelligent video-based movement insight for performance, readiness, and improvement.

Features

  • Capture movement sessions using device camera
  • Analyze form, control, and consistency
  • Identify fatigue, imbalance, and degradation patterns
  • Track session-to-session progress
  • Generate readiness and risk signals
  • Provide clear, actionable movement feedback
  • Maintain visual history and trend reports

Who it’s for

  • Athletes
  • Coaches
  • Rehab and return-to-play monitoring

How it’s used

Record movement sessions, review insights, and track changes over time to support better training, recovery, and performance decisions.
