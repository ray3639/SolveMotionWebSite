Solvelt 5.0 — Interactive criminology stories.

Solvelt takes ideas from criminology, psychology, and decision science and turns them into branching scenarios. You are dropped into a case and asked to decide what to do next.

What you do in Solvelt

  • Read short case files built from realistic situations.
  • Choose how to respond at key decision points.
  • See how bias, evidence, and time pressure change outcomes.

Who it is for

  • Students interested in criminology or criminal justice.
  • People who like detective shows and want to think deeper.
  • Anyone who wants to practice structured thinking using stories.
